These tournaments will run by the following schedule:
First week of month (Until the first sunday after the 5th) - Team signups
Second week of each month (Second sunday after the 5th) - Teams agree on match times
Third week of each month - Round Robin Matches
Fourth week - More roundrobin matches OR placement matches.

To sign up each month costs $25. An email will be sent to the address provided to sort this out.
Each month's prize pool at minimum contains the entirety of the signup money
